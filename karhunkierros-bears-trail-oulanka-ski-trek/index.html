Preview

My first solo multi-day ski trek!

6 days to ski 82 km in the Oulanka national park, in Finland, to try to reach the Arctic Circle and Lapland on Karhunkierros, the Bear's Trail.

No resupply points, only tent campsites in snow or simple wooden huts and lean-to shelters to spend the nights.

First half on a groomed trail, second half fully off-piste.

Freezing temperatures all along in late winter with a phenomenal quantity of snow.

"I am so grateful right now. This is one of those moments."
— Somewhere along the trail (episode 4)

Conditions

Ideal. Cold and sunny the first days, then slowly warming up to just above freezing with clouds and snowfall.

  • Temperature -17ºC to 0.5ºC
  • Altitude 150 m to 400 m
  • Snow cover 85 cm to 150 cm
  • Avalanche risk NA
