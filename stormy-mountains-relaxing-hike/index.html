Relaxing Alpine Hike | Stormy Skies, Cows, and Mountain Views

Conditions

Thunderclouds, warm and windy at times.

  • Temperature 22ºC
  • Altitude 2 050 m

Notes

I was a bit afraid to meet a thunderstorm. Heard thunder in the distance, but it never came too close.

I had an escape plan just in case but hopefully didn't need it.

Those clouds really added a dramatic and beautiful dimension to the views and emphasized the reliefs.
