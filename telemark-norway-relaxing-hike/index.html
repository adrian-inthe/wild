Join me for a relaxing hike in Telemark, Norway, through the forest and above the tree line to the summit to enjoy the astonishing panoramic views over incredible lakes and mountains!

Such a peaceful place and astounding nature.

Watch

Relaxing Hike - Morning hike in Norway (No talking)

Conditions

Bright blue sky, some wind at the top.

  • Temperature 16ºC
  • Altitude 880 m

Notes

Went for a quick hike above the tree line. The weather was perfect. It wasn't warm per se, the early october 16ºC being on the colder side, but the beaming sun was plenty warm.

The ascent brings us to a magnificent panoramic point of vue on top of the local mountain while passing through autumn colored forests, blue and dark creeks and lakes.

Peacefull, relaxing and super cosy atmosphere.
