Preview

Saw an opportunity to test my winter gear!

Hoped on a cable car, put on my new trekking skis and shoved my backpack on my shoulders.

Short glide through a good amount of fresh powder. Set up camp at dusk and then went for an extra ski-stroll under the stars.

The temperature dipped to -16,5ºC this time!
"Well, I learned a lot about my gear on this little expedition!"
— In the morning

Conditions

Ideal: sunny, almost no wind, no precipitations.

  • Temperature -16,5ºC to -12,8ºC
  • Altitude 1 600 m
  • Snow cover 50 cm to 60 cm
  • Avalanche risk level 1 (out of 5)
"I went through the coldest night I ever got so far in a tent!"
— Still under my quilt, hesitating to go out
